Biography

Hailing from Lagos, Nigeria, Jeezi The Lantern is a contemporary R&B sensation redefining the genre. Blending the vocal fluidity of The Weeknd and Chris Brown with the afro-fusion energy of Maleek Berry and Tekno.

His breakout single, "Costain," caught the attention of international tastemakers. The Pit London described the track as an "absolute gem," praising his intricate melodies.
