Biography

Hailing from Lagos, Nigeria, Jeezi The Lantern is a contemporary R&B sensation redefining the genre. Blending the vocal fluidity of The Weeknd and Chris Brown with the afro-fusion energy of Maleek Berry and Tekno, Jeezi creates a sound that is both global and deeply rooted in his Nigerian experience.

His breakout single, "Costain," has already caught the attention of international tastemakers. The Pit London described the track as an "absolute gem," praising Jeezi's ability to weave "intricate melodies and rhythms into a mesmerizing sonic tapestry."

Beyond the music, Jeezi is driven by a mission to address mental health, youth empowerment, and equality. With velvety vocals and an intimate connection to his listeners, Jeezi The Lantern is poised to leave a lasting mark on the R&B scene.
